Exchange Rate Converter
This is an open source exchange rate converter made for rates in bitcoin, satoshis, US-Dollar and Euro. It was created out of the need for a reliable and easy accessible tool as many others are clunky, require many clicks or don't handle satoshi values.
Data Sources
All Bitcoin and Fore price sources are fetched from Alpha Vantage via the free API.
What is a Satoshi (SAT)?
A satoshi is the tiniest fraction of a Bitcoin measurablea at the first layer of the Bitcoin Network. Only at higher layers like the Lightning Network tinier amounts are usable. One bitcoin equals to 100 000 000 satoshis.
Features:
  • Copy price to clipboard on click
  • Load last checked pair on app start
  • Update price by button click or [SPACE]
  • Navigate currencies [← → ↑ ↓]
  • Load menu with [ESCAPE]
